About Me

Haoyue (Frederica) SHI

Greetings! I am a senior undergraduate student from Department of Intelligence Science and Technology, School of EECS, Peking University, with the second major of Sociology.

My research interests include computational linguistics, social computing and deep learning. I also have experiences on machine learning, computer vision and multimedia processing. Specifically, I enjoy studying the problem of understanding (general) language in the world.

I am applying for a Ph.D. program on natural language processing or social computing in the United States. Please see my CV for more detailed information.
